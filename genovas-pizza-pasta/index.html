Genova's Pizza & Pasta

★★★★★

Genova's Pizza & Pasta is Myrtle Beach's home for authentic New York–style pizza and Sicilian squares. Locally owned with family recipes passed down through generations, everything is made fresh daily. Every pizza tells a story of family, tradition, and community. From hand-tossed 18” pies to crispy Sicilian squares and Grandma-style pizza, Genova's brings real New York flavor to the Grand Strand. No frozen shortcuts. No compromises. Just real ingredients and real craft.

The Full Picture

What Makes Genova's Special

◊

Hand-Tossed NY Pies

Available in 10”, 14”, and 18” sizes with over 20 specialty options. The dough is made fresh daily the authentic New York way — thin, crispy edge, perfect chew.

◊

Sicilian & Grandma Style

Thick, pillowy Sicilian squares and Grandma-style pies baked in the pan — a rarer find on the Grand Strand. The Upside Down Sicilian with cheese-first layering is a signature.

◊

Full Italian Menu

Pasta dinners, stromboli, calzones, NY-style hot heroes, wings in 9 flavors, fresh salads, and housemade Italian desserts including cannoli, cheesecake, and rainbow cake.

◊

Gluten-Free Available

Cauliflower crust pizza for guests with gluten sensitivities — made with the same fresh toppings and care as every other pie on the menu.

◊

Community Driven

Genova's runs a monthly Community Impact Program, donating 10% of proceeds to local Myrtle Beach organizations. Pizza that genuinely gives back to the neighborhood.

◊

Catering & Events

Full catering packages from $190 for groups of 10–15. Fresh made daily trays with easy setup. Perfect for parties, corporate events, and large family gatherings.
